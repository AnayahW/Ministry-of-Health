Trinidad and Tobago. We offer a wide range of services to meet the diverse needs of our population.


Health Services

Service Description
Primary Healthcare Access to community health centers and family doctors for regular checkups, diagnosis, and treatment of common illnesses. Preventive care, including vaccinations, screenings, and health education. Family planning and maternal and child health services.
Maternal and Child Health Comprehensive prenatal and postnatal care for expectant and new mothers. Immunization programs for children to protect against preventable diseases. Support for breastfeeding and infant nutrition. Early childhood development programs.
Communicable Disease Control Prevention and control of infectious diseases such as HIV/AIDS, tuberculosis, and Zika. Vaccination campaigns and public health education initiatives. Surveillance and outbreak response programs.
Non-Communicable Disease Management Screening and management of chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and cancer. Lifestyle modification programs, including diet and exercise counseling. Support groups for patients and their families.
Mental Health Promotion of mental well-being and prevention of mental health disorders. Access to counseling services, support groups, and psychiatric care.
